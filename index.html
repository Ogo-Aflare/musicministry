About Me

Minister of The Gospel

OKUNOLA OGOOLUWA, a dedicated gospel minister, born, into a devout Christian home in Lagos, Nigeria. From an early age, I was immersed in the teachings of the Bible, nutured by unwavering faith and example of my parents, who are founders of THE HERALD OF CHRIST'S COMING MINISTRIES, a Bible believing church. At a very young age I started serving as the church instrumentalist, advanced in musical knowledge and learnt to play some musical instruments. I realized my calling into singing ministry briefly after joining and also serving under Beyond Music Network, a body founded by Evangelist Samuel Onwona with the assignment to win souls to the body of Christ through Music. I have by the grace and inspiration of God written some songs which are available on some streaming platforms. My primary assignment as one who has been called into this ministry, is to affect lives, and bring people into the light.
